Meet Hon Alli

Freight Forwarder|Politician

A Man of the People, A Voice for Development

Rotimi is a dedicated community leader, accomplished entrepreneur, and visionary politician committed to transforming Ward E-Igando into a model of sustainable development, security, and prosperity. With a deep-rooted passion for service, Rotimi is running for Councilor to bring accountable leadership, inclusive governance, and tangible progress to every household in our ward.

Life & Education

Rotimi Alli Samson was born on 27th of October; 1989. He is the first of three children and is happily married to Mrs. Eniola Alli. He learned the values of hard work, integrity, and community service from his parents-Dad (Civil Servant), Mum(Trader). He attended Bishop Falope Memorial Nursery and Primary School, Akure, Ondo State and St. John/Mary Unity Secondary School before earning a Diploma in Crime Management, Prevention and Control from Bayero University, Kano and later a Degree in Criminology and Security Studies from Federal University Dutse, Jigawa State. His academic background equipped him with critical problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of Law and Criminal Justice Systems, Forensic Psychology and Criminal Profiling, Intelligence and Counterintelligence, Policy Evaluation and Ethical decision-making.

Professional Excellence & Leadership

With over 5 years of experience in the Freight and Cargo Services Industry, Rotimi has built a reputation as a results-driven leader. As the Founder/CEO of Centigrade Frontline Cargo & Logistics Services Ltd., he has created jobs, empowered youth, and contributed to local economic growth. His leadership role in the Nigerian Association of Freight Forwarders and Consolidators where he currently serves as the National Public Relations Officer further demonstrate his ability to manage resources effectively and deliver impactful results. As someone who has resided in Ward E-Igando for over half a decade, Rotimi has a clear understanding of the myriad developmental challenges in his ward and is prepared to implement actionable solutions to these challenges.
